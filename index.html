The problem
HALA agents are losing merchants in the field
A field agent visits a merchant. They collect info on paper, WhatsApp details to the office, wait for KYC approval, follow up days later. By then the merchant has gone cold, or already signed with a competitor.
Avg onboarding time: 3–5 days. Merchant drop-off: ~40%
